Oil Production Milestone

March 15, 2024 Oil Sector

Iraq has reached a significant milestone in oil production, with daily output exceeding 4.7 million barrels per day (mbpd) in recent months. This achievement marks a strong recovery from the pandemic-induced slowdown and demonstrates the country's growing capacity in the energy sector.

Key Developments

  • Production capacity increased by 15% compared to 2022
  • New infrastructure projects completed in southern fields
  • Enhanced export capabilities through modernized terminals

Economic Impact

The increased production has contributed significantly to government revenue, with oil exports generating over $98 billion in 2023. This has supported economic recovery and enabled increased investment in public services and infrastructure development.

Future Outlook

Iraq aims to further increase production capacity to 5 million bpd by 2025, supported by ongoing infrastructure improvements and international partnerships. The government is also focusing on diversifying export markets and developing downstream industries.
